## Description: 
Melee weapons made of or coated with adamantine are unusually effective when used to break objects. Whenever an adamantine weapon hits an object, the hit is a critical hit.The adamantine version of a melee weapon costs 500 gp more than the normal version, whether the weapon is made of the metal or coated with it.
