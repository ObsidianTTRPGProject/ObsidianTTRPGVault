# Potion of Healing
*Potion, minor, common*  

- **Cost**: 50 gp
- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

You regain 2d4 + 2 hit points when you drink this potion. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 187*
