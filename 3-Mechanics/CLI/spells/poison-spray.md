# Poison Spray
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*cantrip, Conjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 10 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You extend your hand toward a creature you can see within range and project a puff of noxious gas from your palm. The creature must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take `dice: 1d12|avg|noform` (`1d12`) poison damage.

This spell's damage increases by `dice: 1d12|avg|noform` (`1d12`) when you reach 5th level (`dice: 2d12|avg|noform` (`2d12`)), 11th level (`dice: 3d12|avg|noform` (`3d12`)), and 17th level (`dice: 4d12|avg|noform` (`4d12`)).
