# Barkskin
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  
![](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/img/barkskin.webp#right)  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, a handful of oak bark
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target's skin has a rough, bark-like appearance, and the target's AC can't be less than 16, regardless of what kind of armor it is wearing.

**Classes**: [Cleric (Nature Domain)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/cleric-nature-domain.md), [Druid (Circle of the Land)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/druid-circle-of-the-land.md), [Druid](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/druid.md), [Ranger](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/ranger.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 217. Available in the SRD.*
