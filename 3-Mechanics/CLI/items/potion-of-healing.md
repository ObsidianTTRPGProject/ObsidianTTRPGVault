# Potion of Healing
*Potion, minor, common*  

- **Cost**: 50 gp
- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

You regain `dice: 2d4 + 2|avg|noform` (`2d4 + 2`) hit points when you drink this potion. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.
