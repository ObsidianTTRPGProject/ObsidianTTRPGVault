***Multiattack.*** The myrmidon makes three trident attacks.

***Trident.** Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 7 ( `dice: 1d6+4`) piercing damage, or 8 ( `dice: 1d8+4`) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack.

***Freezing Strikes (Recharge 6).*** The myrmidon uses Multiattack. Each attack that hits deals an extra 5 ( `dice: 1d10`) cold damage. A target that is hit by one or more of these attacks has its speed reduced by 10 feet until the end of the myrmidon's next turn.
