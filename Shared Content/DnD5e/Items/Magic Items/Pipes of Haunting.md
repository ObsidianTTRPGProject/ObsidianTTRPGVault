## Description
You must be proficient with wind instruments to use these pipes. They have 3 charges. You can use an action to play them and expend 1 charge to create an eerie, spellbinding tune. Each creature within 30 feet of you that hears you play must succeed on a [[4 - Ability Checks|DC]] 15 [[Wisdom|Wisdom]] saving throw or become [[Frightened|frightened]] of you for 1 minute. A creature that succeeds on its saving throw is immune to the effect of these pipes for 24 hours. The pipes regain `dice: 1d3` (1d3) expended charges daily at dawn.
