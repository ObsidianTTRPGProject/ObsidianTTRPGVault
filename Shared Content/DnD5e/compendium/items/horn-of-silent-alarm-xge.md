# Horn of Silent Alarm
*Wondrous Item, minor, common*  

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

This horn has 4 charges. When you use an action to blow it, one creature of your choice can hear the horns blare, provided the creature is within 600 feet of the horn and not [deafened](/rules/conditions.md#deafened). No other creature hears sound coming from the horn. The horn regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Xanathar's Guide to Everything p. 137*
