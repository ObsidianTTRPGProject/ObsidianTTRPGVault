# Conclusion
*Source: X Marks the Spot, p. 8* 

After retrieving the _Hierophant's Chalice_ from the Temple of Aclazotz, the characters can make their way south to a rendezvous point south of Fort Durron, previously arranged by Alante. They are met by two emissaries from the Order of the Five Sacraments, who fulfill the terms of the various agreements made with the party members.
