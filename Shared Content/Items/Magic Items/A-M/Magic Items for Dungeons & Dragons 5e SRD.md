# Magic Items for Dungeons & Dragons 5e SRD

## Overview
The articles in this grouping comprise the new [[13 - Magic Items|magic items]] introduced in Dungeons & Dragons 5e SRD.

[[13 - Magic Items|Magic items]] are presented in alphabetical order. A magic item’s description gives the item’s name, its category, its rarity, and its magical properties.
